---
title: Build Log 2025
description: A transparency record documenting Netlink’s development progress from August to December 2025.
---


2025 was not focused on growth metrics, market performance, or exposure.  
It was dedicated to establishing the foundational infrastructure of Netlink in a careful and verifiable manner.

This page serves as a chronological public record of development activities carried out during that period.

---

## August 2025

- NET token deployed on the Polygon network  
- Token verification submitted on Polygon

---

## September 2025

- EVM wallet testing  
- Official website launched  
- Social channels activated  
- DEX liquidity pool testing  
- Initial whitepaper published  
- Community airdrop — Phase 1  
- Token officially verified on Polygon explorer

---

## October 2025

- Community airdrop — Phase 2  
- Web3 wallet functionality testing  
- QuickScan audit completed

---

## November 2025

- Netlink MiniApp launched (beta)

---

## December 2025

- 62.04% of total token supply locked  
- Multisig wallet implemented  
- Presale Phase 1 launched via PinkSale  
- Initial documentation published  
- Netlink MiniApp listed on DappRadar

---

## Closing

The work completed in 2025 established the base layer for Netlink as a Web3 infrastructure project.  
Future development will continue to build upon what has already been implemented and made publicly verifiable.

---

## Note on Scope & Intent

This document is published solely as a transparency record of development activities conducted during Netlink’s 2025 build phase.

It is not intended as an investment offer, performance projection, or representation of future outcomes.

Development decisions are made incrementally, with an emphasis on security, sustainability, and long-term usability.  
Features, timelines, and implementations may evolve as the project continues to mature.

This report exists to document progress — not to set expectations beyond what can be verified.
