## 1. Introduction

Netlink Token (NET) is a community-driven digital asset built on the Polygon network. It is designed to support communities, ideas, and real-world projects by providing a simple and transparent way to collaborate, validate initiatives, and build ecosystems together.

NET acts as a bridge between digital communities and real-world participation, enabling individuals and groups to express tangible support, coordinate efforts, and experiment with blockchain-based collaboration without unnecessary technical complexity.

---

## 2. Vision & Mission

### Vision

To build a global, inclusive, and transparent community ecosystem powered by blockchain technology.

### Mission

- Provide simple tools to measure and express support for ideas and projects  
- Serve as a utility token for community funding, rewards, and early access  
- Emphasize transparency and accountability through the Polygon blockchain  

---

## 3. NET Use Cases

NET is designed to support a wide range of community-driven use cases, including:

- **Community Support**  
  Sending NET as a direct and verifiable form of support

- **Early Idea Validation**  
  Validating concepts through real community participation

- **Crowdfunding & Collaboration**  
  Coordinating resources and collective funding for projects

- **Rewards & Appreciation**  
  Recognizing contributors, creators, and active participants

- **Early Access**  
  Providing NET holders with early access to selected initiatives

- **Open Ecosystem Integration**  
  Enabling integration into applications, games, and third-party platforms

---

## 4. Tokenomics

**Total Supply:** 99,000,000 NET

### Token Allocation

- Community: 30%  
- Public & Private Sales: 25%  
- Development: 15%  
- Treasury: 10%  
- Founder & Team: 15%  
- Investor: 5%  

The token distribution is designed to balance community growth, long-term development, and ecosystem sustainability.

## 5. Roadmap

### 2025 — Foundation & Visibility

- Launch of NET on the Polygon network  
- Smart contract deployment and explorer verification  
- Official website and initial whitepaper release  
- Global community airdrop program  
- Initial DEX trial connection (limited liquidity testing)

- Expansion of the global community

- Partnerships with startups and community-led initiatives  
- Introduction of a simple crowdfunding mechanism powered by NET  
- Contributor reward systems

- Evaluation and publication of ecosystem progress since 2025  
- Strengthening NET’s position as a relevant community asset  
- Cross-industry collaborations (startups, communities, creative sectors)

## 6. Governance

As the Netlink ecosystem grows, NET is envisioned to gradually transition toward a decentralized governance model. In its early stages, governance will be facilitated through community discussions, polls, and transparent decision-making processes.

A formal DAO structure may be introduced once the ecosystem and community reach sufficient maturity.
